RNN's and Jupyter Notebook Hints

Posted on Thu 27 April 2017 in events

This week in the beginner session we explored some tips and tricks for getting the most out of Jupyter Notebook. We also looked at how to answer the question, "what libraries are even installed".

In the advanced hour we continued our exploration of RNN's. We examined how you can produce a sequence of outputs from an RNN and discussed how the keras implementation of RNN's works. Next week we will go through LSTM's.
